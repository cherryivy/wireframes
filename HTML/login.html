About Cherry Ivy
Cherry Ivy offers a unique solution to an oft-overlooked problem. That is, how do we engage people in long-term philanthropy? More often than we realize, events such as soup kitchens, park cleanings, galas, coat drives - you name it - are taking place in our local communities. With Cherry Ivyâ€™s event-focused methodology, we aim to bring accessibility to philanthropy. Our goal is to show people that 1) it is extremely easy and convenient to get involved, and 2) that their friends and family are involved as well. Once this concept of accessibility is understood, long-term engagement in philanthropy will be inevitable.
